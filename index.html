About Me

I grew up in Denver, Colorado. I've always had a passion for music and wanted to choose a career that allowed me to use the same creative parts of my brain. I inevitably grew an interest for full stack web development because, like music, it is a language you can use to express yourself and share new ideas with others. I'm currently enrolled in the UCF coding bootcamp and will graduate with a certificate for full stack web development in January of 2024. I'm also working full time as an Information Technology Helpdesk Analyst for Rotech Healthcare. A couple hobbies of mine include playing video games and cooking. I also volunteer at my local church called Illuminate Church in Celebration Florida, playing bass guitar and serving in the youth and young adult ministries.
